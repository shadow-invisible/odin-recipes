Recipe Of Maggi

Ingredients

  • 1 Packet Maggie Noodles
  • 2 Cups Water
  • The Taste maker Packet

Process:

  1. Bring 2 cups of water to a boil in a saucepan.expand_more.
  2. Add the Maggi noodle cake and the taste maker packet.
  3. Cook for 2 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the noodles are cooked through

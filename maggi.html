Recipe Of Maggi

  1. Boil Water
    • Bring 2 cups of water to a boil in a saucepan.
  2. Break Noodles:
    • Break the Maggi noodle cake into four parts and add it to the boiling water.
  3. Cook Noodles:
    • Let the noodles cook for 2 minutes. Stir occasionally to avoid sticking.
  4. Add Seasoning:
    • Once the noodles are soft, add the tastemaker (seasoning) provided in the Maggi packet.
  5. Mix Well:
    • Stir well to evenly distribute the seasoning and cook for another 1-2 minutes until the water reduces to form a slightly thick consistency.
  6. Serve:
    • Remove from heat and let it sit for a minute.
    • Transfer to a bowl and enjoy your hot Maggi noodles!

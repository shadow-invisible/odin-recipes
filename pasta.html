Recipe Of Pasta

Ingredients

  • 8 oz dry pasta (such as spaghetti, penne, or farfalle)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Process:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package directions in a large pot of salted water. Reserve about 1/2 cup of the pasta water before draining.
  2. While the pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and cook for about 30 seconds, until fragrant. Be careful not to burn the garlic!
  3. Drain the pasta and add it to the skillet with the garlic and olive oil. Toss to coat.
  4. Add the Parmesan cheese and reserved pasta water to the skillet. Toss everything together until the cheese is melted and the sauce coats the pasta. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
